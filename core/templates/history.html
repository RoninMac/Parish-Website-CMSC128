Our Parish History

The Virgen Dela Candelaria Parish was established in the early 1900s, rooted in a deep devotion to the Blessed Virgin Mary. Over the decades, our parish has grown into a vibrant community of faith, serving as a spiritual home for countless families.

Milestones

  • 1905: Founding of the parish by Spanish missionaries.
  • 1920: Construction of the original church building.
  • 1950: Expansion of the parish to include a school and community center.
  • 2000: Major renovation of the church to accommodate a growing congregation.
  • 2020: Launch of our online ministry to reach parishioners during the pandemic.

Our Mission

Throughout our history, our mission has remained steadfast: to foster a welcoming environment where all can grow in faith, serve others, and celebrate the sacraments. We are committed to continuing this legacy for future generations.
